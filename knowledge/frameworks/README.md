# Frameworks Layer

Strategic models and analytical tools. Documents here are synthesized originals — not summaries of copyrighted books, but the operative frameworks extracted and made promptable.

## Planned documents

- `frameworks-reference.md` — In progress. Operative models from:
  - Rumelt — Good Strategy/Bad Strategy (kernel model, proximate objectives)
  - Lafley & Martin — Playing to Win (strategy cascade, where-to-play/how-to-win)
  - April Dunford — Obviously Awesome (competitive alternatives, unique attributes)
  - Byron Sharp — How Brands Grow (mental/physical availability, distinctive assets)
  - Kahneman — Thinking Fast and Slow (System 1/2, cognitive biases in decision-making)
  - Ulwick — Jobs to Be Done (outcome-driven innovation, job mapping)
  - Cagan — Inspired (product discovery, opportunity assessment)
  - Morgan — Eating the Big Fish (challenger brand strategy)

## Update frequency
Periodic — as frameworks are synthesized and added.

## Ingestion command
`npm run ingest:frameworks` (once document is ready)
